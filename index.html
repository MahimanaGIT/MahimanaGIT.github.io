About Me

I am a snatak (a.k.a. Graduate Student) in Robotics Engineering at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. My areas of research are Computer Vision , Deep Learning and Robotics. I am also a member of Rho Beta Epsilon Robotics Honor Society at WPI.

I am currently a research intern at Institute for Human and Machine Cognition (IHMC) working on an Autonomous Ground Vehicle (AGV) from Army Research Laborator (ARL). Before starting my masters at Worcester Polytechnic Institute, I was working at Addverb Technologies, a warehouse automation company where I worked on multiple projects suchs as Forklift Tracking System, Pick To Light, Process Monitoring System etc. which included programming microcontrollers, single board computers such as Raspberry Pi, Asus Tinkerboard etc.

I did my undergrad from Shri Govindram Seksaria Institute of Technology and Science in Electronics and Telecommunication where I was the Chief Technology Officer of the Robotics Club. Being a student and active member of Robotics Club, I developed and mentored many projects. I like to participate in robotics competitios, in the past, I participated in E-Yantra Robotics Competition (2016, 2017), Texas Instruments Innovation Challenge (2016) and recently participated in NASA Space Robotics Challenge with WPI.
